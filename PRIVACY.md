# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 2026

## Overview

Atlas ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how it is used.

## Data We Collect

- **Goals and dreams**: Title, description, category, target date, location, and images you add
- **Profile information**: Name and profile photo you set during onboarding
- **Location data**: Geographic coordinates and place names you choose for goals (not your current GPS location unless you explicitly share it)
- **Usage analytics**: Anonymous events like goal creation, completion, and screen views (no personal info)

## How Your Data Is Stored

- All data is stored in **Supabase**, a secure cloud database with end-to-end encryption in transit (TLS/SSL)
- Images are stored in Supabase Storage
- Authentication is handled by Supabase Auth (email/password)
- Your data is associated with your account and not shared with other users

## Third-Party Services

- **Supabase** (supabase.com) — database, authentication, storage
- **Sentry** (sentry.io) — crash reporting and error tracking (anonymous)
- **PostHog** (posthog.com) — anonymous product analytics (no PII)

## Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account exists. You can delete all data at any time from **Settings → Delete Account**.

## Your Rights

- Access your data: all data is visible in the app
- Delete your data: Settings → Delete Account
- Export: contact us at the email below
